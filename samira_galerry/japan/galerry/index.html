Japan

a country with a unique culture and centuries-old traditions that are carefully passed down from generation to generation. This country is in many ways ahead of our time and at the same time does not lose its originality. Ancient temples and medieval castles coexist with skyscrapers and superhighways. But look into the tea house... and you will experience something that the latest technology will never provide. Traveling to Japan is a journey into the past and the future at the same time.
